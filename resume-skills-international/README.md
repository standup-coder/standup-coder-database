## Key International Considerations

### Regional Resume Formats
- **North America**: Chronological, achievement-focused
- **Europe**: CV-style, comprehensive education details
- **Asia-Pacific**: Hybrid formats, relationship emphasis
- **Middle East**: Formal structure, extensive personal information

## Best Practices

### Content Strategy
1. Research target market expectations
2. Customize for specific industries and roles
3. Quantify achievements with measurable results
4. Use active voice and strong action verbs

### Format and Design
1. Clean, professional layout
2. Consistent formatting and typography
3. Appropriate length for target region
4. Mobile-friendly and ATS-optimized

### Cultural Sensitivity
1. Avoid culturally inappropriate content
2. Adapt professional photo requirements
3. Consider religious and personal information inclusion
4. Adjust contact information presentation
