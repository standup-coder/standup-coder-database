# 国际科技基金会名录

## 1. 项目介绍
本名录收录主要的国际科技基金会、科研资助组织和创新支持机构，涵盖政府支持、企业设立和私人基金会等来自世界各地的机构。它为寻求资金和资源对接的研究人员、创新者和技术团队提供权威参考。

## 2. 基金会分类

### 2.1 政府支持基金会
由政府部门或机构设立，主要支持国家重大科技项目和基础研究。

### 2.2 企业基金会
由领先企业或企业家创立，专注于产学研合作和产业化应用。

### 2.3 私人慈善基金会
由社会组织或个人发起，致力于促进技术创新和社会进步。

### 2.4 国际合作基金会
促进国际科技交流，支持全球研究项目。

## 3. 主要国际基金会

### 3.1 国家科学基金会(NSF) - 美国
- **成立时间**：1950年
- **管理机构**：美国国家科学基金会
- **使命**：促进科学发展；推进国家健康、繁荣和福利
- **主要项目**：
  - 研究资助（基础研究支持）
  - 研究生研究奖学金计划（支持研究生）
  - 教师早期职业发展(CAREER)计划
  - 创新军团(I-Corps)计划（技术转化）
- **资助范围**：1万美元 - 300万美元
- **申请周期**：滚动提交，有特定截止日期
- **网站**：https://www.nsf.gov

### 3.2 欧洲研究理事会(ERC)
- **成立时间**：2007年
- **管理机构**：欧盟
- **使命**：支持所有领域的前沿研究
- **主要项目**：
  - 启动资助(早期研究人员)
  - 整合资助(成熟研究人员)
  - 高级资助(领先研究人员)
  - 协同资助(合作项目)
- **资助范围**：15万欧元 - 1000万欧元
- **网站**：https://erc.europa.eu

### 3.3 比尔·梅琳达·盖茨基金会
- **成立时间**：2000年
- **创始人**：比尔·盖茨和梅琳达·弗伦奇·盖茨
- **使命**：在全球范围内改善医疗保健并减少极端贫困
- **Major Programs**:
  - Grand Challenges Explorations (innovative global health solutions)
  - Global Health Drug Discovery
  - Agricultural Development Initiative
  - Education Technology Grants
- **Funding Range**: $100,000 - $50 million
- **Website**: https://www.gatesfoundation.org

### 3.4 Wellcome Trust - United Kingdom
- **Established**: 1936
- **Type**: Independent charitable foundation
- **Mission**: Improve health by supporting science to solve urgent problems
- **Major Programs**:
  - Investigator Awards (supporting outstanding researchers)
  - Discovery Awards (high-risk, high-reward research)
  - Innovation Fellowships
  - Global Health Innovation Challenges
- **Funding Range**: £25,000 - £10 million
- **Website**: https://wellcome.org

### 3.5 Japan Science and Technology Agency (JST)
- **Established**: 1957
- **Governing Body**: Japanese Government
- **Mission**: Advance science and technology for societal benefit
- **Major Programs**:
  - CREST (Core Research for Evolutional Science and Technology)
  - PRESTO (Precursory Research for Embryonic Science and Technology)
  - Moonshot R&D Program
  - International Joint Research Program
- **Funding Range**: ¥10 million - ¥500 million
- **Website**: https://www.jst.go.jp

### 3.6 Max Planck Society - Germany
- **Established**: 1948
- **Type**: Non-university research organization
- **Mission**: Conduct basic research in natural sciences, life sciences, social sciences, and humanities
- **Major Programs**:
  - Max Planck Research Groups
  - International Max Planck Research Schools
  - Partner Group Program
  - Alumni Network Support
- **Funding Range**: €200,000 - €2 million annually
- **Website**: https://www.mpg.de

### 3.7 Chan Zuckerberg Initiative
- **Established**: 2015
- **Founders**: Mark Zuckerberg and Priscilla Chan
- **Mission**: Advance human potential and promote equality through science and technology
- **Major Programs**:
  - Biohub Network (collaborative biomedical research)
  - Essential Open Source Software for Science
  - Imaging Scientists Program
  - Science Policy Fellowship
- **Funding Range**: $500,000 - $50 million
- **Website**: https://chanzuckerberg.com

### 3.8 Australian Research Council (ARC)
- **Established**: 1965
- **Governing Body**: Australian Government
- **Mission**: Support excellent research and innovation
- **Major Programs**:
  - Discovery Projects (fundamental research)
  - Linkage Projects (industry collaboration)
  - Future Fellowships (mid-career researchers)
  - Laureate Fellowships (outstanding researchers)
- **Funding Range**: AUD 50,000 - AUD 3 million
- **Website**: https://www.arc.gov.au

### 3.9 Korea Institute of Science and Technology Information (KISTI)
- **Established**: 1962
- **Governing Body**: Korean Government
- **Mission**: Support national science and technology development
- **Major Programs**:
  - Basic Research Support Program
  - Convergent Research Program
  - International Collaboration Projects
  - Young Scientist Support Program
- **Funding Range**: KRW 50 million - KRW 5 billion
- **Website**: https://www.kisti.re.kr

### 3.10 Israel Innovation Authority
- **Established**: 1984
- **Governing Body**: Israeli Government
- **Mission**: Promote industrial R&D and technological innovation
- **Major Programs**:
  - Magnet Program (industry-academia collaboration)
  - Kamin Program (international R&D cooperation)
  - Start-Up Nation Central (entrepreneurship support)
  - Technology Incubators Program
- **Funding Range**: $50,000 - $2 million
- **Website**: https://innovationisrael.org.il

## 4. Application Guidelines and Best Practices

### 4.1 Pre-Application Preparation
- **Project Positioning**: Clearly define research direction and innovation points
- **Team Formation**: Assemble capable research teams
- **Budget Planning**: Develop realistic project budgets
- **Timeline Development**: Create detailed research schedules

### 4.2 Application Materials
- **Research Proposal**: Comprehensive description of research content, objectives, and significance
- **Technical Approach**: Specific methodology and implementation plan
- **Team Profiles**: CVs and research backgrounds of core members
- **Expected Outcomes**: Clear deliverables and evaluation criteria

### 4.3 Evaluation Criteria
- **Innovation**: Originality and advancement of the proposed work
- **Feasibility**: Technical viability and team capability
- **Impact**: Potential applications and societal benefits
- **Scientific Merit**: Contribution to advancing knowledge

### 4.4 Post-Award Management
- **Progress Reporting**: Regular submission of project status updates
- **Financial Oversight**: Strict adherence to budget guidelines
- **Results Dissemination**: Timely publication and knowledge sharing
- **Final Assessment**: Participation in project evaluation processes
