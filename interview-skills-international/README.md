### Cultural Adaptation

- Professional dress codes and body language norms
- Time management and punctuality standards

### Language Proficiency
- English proficiency requirements
- Technical terminology in different contexts

### Regional Variations
- **North America**: Direct communication, achievement-focused
- **Europe**: Collaborative approach, work-life balance emphasis
- **Asia-Pacific**: Hierarchical respect, relationship-building
- **Middle East**: Relationship-first approach, formal protocols

## Preparation Strategies

### Pre-Interview Research
1. Company culture and values in target region
2. Typical interview processes and formats
3. Common behavioral question patterns
4. Technical stack and industry standards

### Practice Methods
1. Mock interviews with native speakers
2. Cultural scenario preparation
3. Technical problem-solving in English
4. Virtual interview platform familiarity

- Thank you note customs by region
- Timeline expectations for responses
- Negotiation styles and expectations
- Acceptance and onboarding procedures
